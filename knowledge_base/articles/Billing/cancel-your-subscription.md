# Cancel your subscription

## How to cancel

1. Open **Settings → Billing → Plan**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription** and confirm.
3. The plan stays active until the **end of the current billing period** — no
   further charges after that.

## What happens to files

- At the end of the period the account moves to the **Free** plan (5 GB).
- If stored data exceeds **5 GB**, files become **read-only**: the customer can
  view, download and delete, but not add new files until under the limit.
- Files are **never automatically deleted** on cancellation.

## Reactivating

Re-subscribe any time from **Settings → Billing**. Read-only files become
editable again as soon as the paid plan is active.

> Want money back for the latest charge too? Check [Request a refund](request-a-refund.md)
> for the 14-day eligibility window.
