# Request a refund

## Eligibility

- Refunds are available within **14 days** of a charge.
- Applies to the **most recent** payment only.
- Annual plans are eligible for a **prorated** refund within the 14-day window.

## How to process

1. Confirm the charge date in **Settings → Billing → History**.
2. Verify it falls within the **14-day** window.
3. Submit the refund in the billing console with a reason code.
4. Refunds return to the original payment method in **5–10 business days**.

## Not eligible?

If the charge is older than 14 days, a refund generally cannot be issued. Offer
to [cancel the subscription](cancel-your-subscription.md) so the customer isn't
billed again, and escalate to a supervisor if they dispute the policy.

> Always confirm the customer's identity on the account before discussing or
> issuing a refund.
