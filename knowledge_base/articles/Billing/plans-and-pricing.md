# Plans and pricing

| Plan | Storage | Price (monthly) | Price (yearly) | Users |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Free | **5 GB** | $0 | $0 | 1 |
| Plus | **2 TB** | **$9.99** | **$99/yr** (2 months free) | 1 |
| Family | **6 TB** | **$19.99** | **$199/yr** | up to 5 |
| Business | **5 TB/user** | **$15/user** | **$150/user/yr** | 3+ |

## What's included

- **Free** — basic sync and sharing, 30-day file history.
- **Plus** — everything in Free, **180-day** file history, larger upload limits.
- **Family** — Plus features for up to 5 people, each with a private space.
- **Business** — admin console, audit log, and **365-day** file history.

## Changing plans

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately; the new price is **prorated** for the
  current period.
- **Downgrades** take effect at the **next renewal date**. The account must be
  under the new plan's storage limit first.
