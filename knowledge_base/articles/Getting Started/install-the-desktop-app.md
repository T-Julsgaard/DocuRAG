# Install the desktop app

The desktop app creates a **Nimbus folder** on your computer. Anything you put in
it syncs to the cloud and your other devices.

## Install

1. Go to **nimbus.example/download** and choose your operating system.
2. Run the installer and follow the prompts.
3. Sign in with your Nimbus email and password.
4. Choose where to put your Nimbus folder (default: your home directory).

## Supported systems

| OS | Minimum version |
|---|---|
| Windows | 10 or later |
| macOS | 12 (Monterey) or later |
| Linux | Ubuntu 20.04+ / Fedora 36+ |

## Selective sync

To save disk space, open **Settings → Sync** and uncheck folders you don't want
stored locally. They stay in the cloud and remain accessible from the web app.

If files don't appear after install, see [Files not syncing](../Troubleshooting/files-not-syncing.md).
