# How we protect your data

## Encryption

- Files are encrypted **in transit** (TLS 1.2+) and **at rest** (AES-256).
- Encryption keys are managed by Nimbus and rotated regularly.

## Access and privacy

- Only the account owner and people they share with can access their files.
- Nimbus staff cannot read file contents; support can see file **names and
  metadata** only when needed to resolve a ticket, and only with your consent.

## Data residency

Customer data is stored in the region selected at signup: **EU** or **US**.
The region cannot be changed after the account is created.

## Deleting your data

- Deleting a file moves it to **Trash**, where it's recoverable for **30 days**
  before permanent removal.
- Deleting the **account** removes all files and personal data within **30 days**.
  This is irreversible — confirm identity before processing such a request.

> For formal data-export or erasure requests (e.g. GDPR), route the customer to
> **privacy@nimbus.example** and log the request.
