# Change your email address

1. Open **Settings → Account → Email**.
2. Enter the new email address and your current password.
3. We send a confirmation link to the **new** address — it's valid for **24 hours**.
4. The change takes effect once the customer clicks the link.

A notification is also sent to the **old** address. If the customer didn't make
the change, they should reset the password immediately and contact support.

## Notes

- Sign-in uses the new email straight after confirmation.
- Shared links and collaborators are unaffected.
- The email on file for **billing receipts** updates automatically too.
