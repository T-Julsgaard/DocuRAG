# Reset your password

## If you're signed out

1. On the sign-in page, click **Forgot password?**
2. Enter your account email and submit.
3. Open the reset email and click **Reset password** (the link is valid for **1 hour**).
4. Choose a new password — minimum **10 characters**, with at least one number.

## If you're signed in

Open **Settings → Security → Change password**. You'll need your current password.

## Reset email didn't arrive

- Check the spam/junk folder.
- Confirm the email matches the one on the account (try alternate addresses).
- Wait **5 minutes** — delivery can be delayed.
- Still nothing? The address may not have a Nimbus account. Have the customer
  confirm their signup email.

> Resetting a password does **not** sign out other devices. To do that, use
> **Settings → Security → Sign out all sessions**.
